Rootch?

  • Presentations like Keynote or Powerpoint in the browser
  • Works with jQuery and CSS3
  • Tested in recent browsers
  • Uses the keyboard or scroll wheel to navigate through the slides
    • RIGHT next slide
    • LEFT previous slide
    • H first slide
    • E last slide
    • S show all items on all slides
